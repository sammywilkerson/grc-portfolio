# GRC Portfolio

This portfolio is for hiring managers and teams seeking a candidate who can translate technical risk into business outcomes.

It supports roles in Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC), Security Compliance, Risk Analysis, and audit readiness.

Included artifacts:
- Risk Register
- Control Assessment
- Vendor Risk Review
- SOC 2 Gap Analysis
- Sample Policies

Frameworks used:
SOC 2, NIST CSF, CIS Controls

SecAI / AI governance is included to reflect emerging risk areas where data integrity, model security, and responsible AI use are becoming critical to modern GRC programs.
