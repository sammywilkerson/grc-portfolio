# Risk Register

This artifact is part of a GRC portfolio project based on a realistic SaaS company running in AWS and handling customer data.

I created this risk register to practice identifying and prioritizing real-world risks in a cloud environment. The focus was on translating technical issues into business impact, ownership, and actionable treatment plans.

The scenario reflects common cloud realities: IAM in AWS, logging and monitoring gaps, vendor dependencies, and backup/recovery risks.

Each row represents:
- what asset or process is being evaluated  
- what the actual risk is (cause → impact)  
- how likely it is to occur  
- how severe the business impact would be  
- who is responsible for addressing it  
- what action should be taken  

I also included scoring and control references (NIST, CIS, SOC 2) to show how each risk connects to broader governance and compliance efforts.

This project helped me practice thinking through risk the way a security or GRC team would—prioritizing what matters most and aligning technical concerns to business outcomes.
