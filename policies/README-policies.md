# Policies

This artifact is part of a GRC portfolio project based on a realistic SaaS company running in AWS and handling customer data.

These policies were created to demonstrate how governance is documented across core areas including acceptable use and asset management, incident response, customer data handling, vendor management, and AI use.

The goal was to show how policies guide behavior, reduce risk, and align with frameworks like SOC 2, NIST CSF, and CIS Controls.

The AI Use Policy is included to reflect emerging governance needs around data protection, responsible AI usage, model oversight, and third-party AI services in modern environments.

These files are intended as portfolio-ready policy samples rather than a full operational enterprise policy library.
