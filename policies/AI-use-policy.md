# AI Use Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines how artificial intelligence (AI) tools are used within the organization to ensure security, privacy, and responsible use.

## Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and systems using AI technologies.

## Policy Statements
- AI tools must not be used to process sensitive customer data unless approved.
- Outputs from AI systems must be reviewed before use in production or decision-making.
- AI-generated code must go through standard security and code review processes.
- Only approved AI vendors and services may be used.
- Users must not input confidential or proprietary data into public AI tools.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- Security: Define safeguards and monitor usage
- GRC: Ensure compliance and policy alignment
- Users: Follow policy and report misuse

## Review
Policy reviewed annually or upon major AI adoption changes.
